VARANASI
The ancient city where spirituality flows along the sacred Ganges

One of the oldest living inhabitations of the world, the sacred city of Varanasi or Benaras, nestled on the banks

of the holy Ganges river, has been attracting pilgrims for centuries. Celebrated author Mark Twain,

who visited India in the last decade of the 19th century, described the city as

“older than history, older than tradition, older even than legend.”
