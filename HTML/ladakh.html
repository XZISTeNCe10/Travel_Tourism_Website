LADAKH
Where rugged landscapes meet serene beauty

Ladakh, nestled in the northernmost reaches of India, is a land of breathtaking beauty and cultural richness.

Its tourism is characterized by stunning high-altitude landscapes, including dramatic mountain ranges,

crystal-clear lakes like Pangong and Tso Moriri, and ancient monasteries perched on rugged cliffs. Visitors flock

to Ladakh for its adventurous trekking and mountaineering opportunities, as well as for the chance to experience

its unique Tibetan-Buddhist culture. From the vibrant markets of Leh to the ambience of remote villages.
